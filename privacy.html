privacy

last updated: 2025

SPITE has no servers, no analytics, no cookies, no email list, and no third-party tracking.

All data you enter — grudges, notes, screenshots, settings — is stored in your browser's localStorage on the device you're using. It never leaves your device. We have no way to see it, recover it, or back it up. Clear your browser data and it's gone.

SPITE makes exactly two kinds of network requests:

  1. Loading the static site itself (HTML/CSS/JS/images) from our CDN.
  2. When (and only when) you paste a license key into the unlock dialog, the app sends a single CORS POST to https://api.gumroad.com/v2/licenses/verify containing the product ID and the key. Gumroad's response tells us whether to unlock Pro on this device. We never see this request — it goes from your browser directly to Gumroad.

If you buy Pro, the purchase itself is processed by Gumroad. Gumroad is the merchant of record and has its own privacy policy at gumroad.com/privacy. The only thing SPITE stores from a purchase is the license key you paste in, which is kept in your browser's localStorage so the app stays unlocked between sessions.

That's the whole policy. There is no "we may share your data with affiliates". There's no data to share.
