frequently asked, mostly suspicious

is this serious or a joke?

both. SPITE is funny on purpose, but the underlying technique — naming and filing a grievance to shrink it — is straight CBT. we just put it in a black t-shirt.

where is my data stored?

in your browser's localStorage. on this device. SPITE has no servers, no analytics, no email list, no auth. close the tab — it stays. open it tomorrow — it's still here. clear your browser data — it's gone.

so if I switch devices, do I lose my grudges?

yes — unless you export the JSON (top bar → export) and import it on the other device. Pro will add encrypted cloud sync via magic link as the next feature.

does the watermark really come off with Pro?

yes. unlock Pro → re-generate the receipt card → no watermark. your previously generated cards keep theirs (they were already saved as PNG).

how does the $9 license work?

buy on Gumroad → Gumroad emails you a license key → paste it into SPITE → unlocked, on this device, forever. you can paste the same key on any number of your own devices. one key, no account.

refunds?

seven days, no questions asked, processed by Gumroad. we don't have a server to ask you on anyway.

can I sync between phone and laptop?

not yet — that's the headline Pro feature shipping next. the Pro license you buy today will include it when it ships, no upcharge.

is this a real business or a side project?

indie. one person, one strong opinion, zero VC money. that's why it costs $9 once instead of $19/mo forever.
